My Process
Every brand & every client is unique, and good visual communication is an ever evolving process. I will provide a tailored solution to the unique challenges of your business, by following proven development best practices, leveraging the best technologies & tools available.
Briefing

Our first step is always getting to know each other & talking about needs & dreams. I'll work out your exact requirements & put together a service package & timeline that fits your needs.

Design

I will send you drafts of my work so you can check if it fits your vision & caters to your needs. Tweaks will be made according to your feedback, until I achieve the final desired solution.

Delivery

In the end I deliver the final product. No matter the medium, I make sure the finished project is up to quality standards and that you have everything you need.

Services
Here's what we can work on together.
Web Development

Website development is at the core of what I offer. I build bespoke websites that can drive your business, improve sales, increase conversion, engage your customers & build your brand.

UI Design

User interface (UI) design is the process of building interfaces in software & the internet, focusing on looks & style. I create interfaces that you'll find easy & intuitive to use.

UX Design

User experience (UX) design is putting users goals & expectations at the forefront of building digital services. It is vital to all projects & businesses, and good UX keeps users engaged.

SEO Audit

You have a beautiful website with useful content but you're not getting seen. I analyse websites & highlight actionable steps to improve their performance & search rankings.

Maintenance

Keeping your website up-to-date is a vital part of running a business. It can save you money in the long run, as problems or security issues can be fixed pro-actively, rather than reactively.

Training

You have a new website or inherited a WordPress site, but you don't really know where to start. I can provide WordPress training that is tailored for you and your team.
