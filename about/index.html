How It Began
I've always been a tinkerer. Always curious to know how things work, why they're designed the way they are & how to make them better. Having been exposed to computers at an early age, I got fascinated by the software they ran, and discovering the internet & all that it entails only got me deeper into tech, in both my personal & professional life.
How It's Going
After completing my degree in Computer Science, I decided to focus on digital design, creating the interfaces that people use on the internet. Working under the studio name Auxilia, I help my clients bring their ideas to life by creating engaging digital experiences & strategies.
Web Development

Website development is at the core of what I offer. I build bespoke websites that can drive your business, improve sales, increase conversion, engage your customers & build your brand.

UI Design

User interface (UI) design is the process of building interfaces in software or computerized devices, focusing on looks and style. I create interfaces that you'll find easy and intuitive to use.

UX Design

User experience (UX) design is putting users goals & expectations at the forefront of building digital services. It is vital to all projects & businesses, and good UX keeps users engaged.

SEO Audit

You have a beautiful website with useful content but you're not getting seen. I analyse websites and highlight actionable steps to improve your website & search rankings.

Maintenance

Keeping your website up-to-date is a vital part of running a business. It can save you money in the long run, as problems or security issues can be fixed pro-actively, rather than reactively.

Training

You have a new website or inherited a WordPress site, but you don't really know where to start. I can provide WordPress training that is tailored for you and your team.
