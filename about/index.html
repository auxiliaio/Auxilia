How It Began
I've always been a tinkerer. Always curious to know how things work, why they're designed the way they are & how to make them better. Having been exposed to computers at an early age, I got fascinated by the software they ran, and discovering the internet & all that it entails only got me deeper into tech, in both my personal & professional life.
How It's Going
After completing my degree in Computer Science, I decided to focus on digital design, creating the interfaces that people use on the internet. Working under the studio name Auxilia, I help my clients bring their ideas to life by creating engaging digital experiences & strategies.
