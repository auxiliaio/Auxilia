Classic Steel Engineering
CSE specialises in tailor made stainless steel fabrication and commercial food & beverage equipment solutions.
Web Design

Client

Classic Steel Engineering Ltd provides solutions and products in operation across multiple industries and industry segments including Corporate, Banking, Government institutions, Education, manufacturing, non-governmental institutions and Hospitality.

Challenge

I was tasked with designing a new website from scratch to establish their presence online. I chose to use WordPress due to it’s versatility and popularity as a content management system. The main goal of the website was to provide information on the company’s services, products and work processes.

Process

First, I interviewed the founder to get a deeper understanding of the company’s identity. After gathering all the info from the interview, like what they’re doing, how, why, and who is involved as well as their values, I looked into how best to portray this to their customers.

Solution

I provided the client with 3 WordPress themes to choose from. They chose Sidney with a custom menu design.

Base Website Theme.

The theme integrates well with the Elementor page builder, which was especially instrumental in getting the custom menu design exactly the way the client wanted it. The company’s brand colors were maintained, and two new fonts - Montserrat and Cairo - were chosen for the website copy.

After feedback rounds, testing and iterations, I finally delivered the website highlighted below.

Desktop & Mobile Homepage.

Services.

About & Products.

Results

The new website was launched successfully and provided CSE with a solid base on which to build their online presence and get found by current & future customers. Their clients also appreciated the easy to read pages as well as the detailed map & contact information available.
